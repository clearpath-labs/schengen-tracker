Frequently Asked Questions

What is the 90/180-day rule?

The Schengen zone allows visa-free travelers to stay for up to 90 days within any rolling 180-day period. Schengen Tracker helps you keep count across multiple trips so you always know how many days you have left.

Where is my data stored?

All of your travel data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to a server or cloud service. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

Why does the app ask to track my activity?

Schengen Tracker uses Google AdMob to display ads. The tracking permission allows AdMob to show you more relevant ads. You can decline this permission and still use the app normally — you will simply see non-personalized ads instead.

How do I reset my data?

You can delete the app and reinstall it to start fresh. Since all data is stored on your device, reinstalling gives you a clean slate.
